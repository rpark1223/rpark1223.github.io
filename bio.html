Robin Park's Biography

Robin Park (born 23 December 1998) is currently a student in his third year at Tufts University in Medford, MA, where he is studying Computer Science and Cognitive and Brain Sciences. Robin was born to two immigrant parents from Korea and spent the first years of his life living in Douglaston, NY. He moved to Great Neck prior to attending primary school at Lakeville Elementarty School, and resided in Great Neck throughout his middle and high school years at Great Neck South Middle and Great Neck South High School, respectively. Robin enjoys reading, listening to music, playing basketball, eating, and sleeping.
